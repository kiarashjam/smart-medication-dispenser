## Executive Summary

The European smart medication dispenser market represents a **€2.26 billion opportunity by 2032**, driven by an aging population, rising chronic disease prevalence, and healthcare cost pressures. Switzerland, with the **highest healthcare spending per capita in Europe** and a **tech-savvy senior population**, provides the ideal launch market before expanding across DACH and broader Europe.

| Key Insight | Value | Business Implication |
|:------------|:------|:---------------------|
| European market (2032) | €2.26 billion | Large TAM for scale |
| Swiss market (2032) | CHF 250 million | Premium launch market |
| CAGR (Europe) | 10.2% | Sustained growth opportunity |
| Swiss 65+ population | 1.72 million | 600K+ target households |
| Medication non-adherence cost (CH) | CHF 2.5B/year | Clear value proposition |
| Seniors on 5+ medications (CH) | 610,000 | Direct target audience |
