## Executive Summary

The European smart medication dispenser market is **fragmented with no clear leader**, creating a compelling market entry opportunity. Our competitive analysis identifies critical gaps that position us for market leadership:

| Strategic Gap | Market Reality | Our Advantage |
|:--------------|:---------------|:--------------|
| **No travel solution** | 35% of Swiss seniors travel; zero competitors serve them | Only provider with portable device |
| **B2B underserved** | Pharmacies want digital tools; competitors offer limited APIs | Purpose-built B2B platform |
| **Swiss market vacant** | No strong local player; CHF 120M market | First-mover in home market |
| **Premium pricing** | Philips at €55+/mo, Medido at €45/mo | 20-35% below major competitors |
| **Multi-language gap** | No competitor supports all 4 Swiss languages | Native FR/DE/IT/EN |

**Competitive Position:** We enter as the only player combining home + travel devices, strong B2B platform, Swiss quality positioning, and competitive pricing.
