Audit trails, fiscal periods, and data isolation — ready for any tax review

Financial data demands the highest level of protection. Finance Pro is built with compliance-first architecture — every action tracked, every period lockable, every organization isolated.

🔒
Audit Trail

Every action logged: who, when, what changed, IP address.

📅
Fiscal Periods

🏢
Data Isolation

Multi-tenant at DB level. Zero cross-org leakage.

Tax Authority Ready: When the Swiss tax authority reviews the books, Finance Pro provides audit logs, locked fiscal periods, and balanced double-entry bookkeeping.
